About SMC SRC

The Student Research Forum at Sargodha Medical College is dedicated to fostering a culture of scientific inquiry and innovation among medical students. We provide a platform for students to engage in cutting-edge research, collaborate with faculty mentors, and present their findings at national and international conferences.

Our mission is to empower the next generation of medical researchers and clinicians through hands-on research experience, specialized training workshops, and interdisciplinary collaboration. We believe that research is fundamental to advancing medical knowledge and improving patient care.

Frequently Asked Questions

What are the admission requirements for SMC? +

Admission to SMC requires completion of intermediate education with pre-medical subjects (Biology, Chemistry, Physics) with a minimum 60% marks. Candidates must also pass the entrance examination and interview.

What facilities are available for students? +

SMC offers state-of-the-art laboratories, modern classrooms, a comprehensive library, hostel accommodation, sports facilities, and 24/7 medical services for all students.

Are there scholarship opportunities available? +

Yes, SMC offers various scholarship programs based on academic merit, financial need, and special achievements. Details are available in our admissions office.

What is the duration of the medical program? +

The MBBS program at SMC is a 5-year degree program followed by a mandatory 1-year house job, making it a total of 6 years to complete medical education.
