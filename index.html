Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common queries about our programs

How do I apply for the MBBS program?

Applications for the MBBS program are accepted through our online portal. You'll need to submit your academic transcripts, entrance exam scores, and a personal statement. The application period typically opens in January each year.

What are the scholarship opportunities?

We offer merit-based scholarships covering up to 100% of tuition fees for outstanding students. Need-based financial aid is also available. Detailed information can be found in the financial aid section of our website.

What facilities are available on campus?

Our campus features state-of-the-art laboratories, a comprehensive medical library, modern lecture halls, student accommodation, sports facilities, and a teaching hospital with over 500 beds.

Is hostel accommodation available?

Yes, we provide separate hostel facilities for male and female students. The hostels are equipped with modern amenities including Wi-Fi, study rooms, recreational areas, and 24/7 security.
