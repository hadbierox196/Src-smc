Research Domain

Letter to the Editor

Short, focused communications that highlight timely clinical observations, responses to published work, or concise evidence summaries. Ideal for students to practice brief scientific writing and rapid dissemination.

Cross-Sectional Studies

Design and analysis of cross-sectional research exploring prevalence and associations. Mentorship available for study design, sampling, data collection, and interpretation of results.

Meta-Analysis

Guidance on systematic review methodology, pooling data, and interpreting heterogeneity. We support literature search strategies, extraction, and meta-analytic tools.

CDC WONDER

Training on using public datasets such as CDC WONDER for epidemiologic analyses, mortality trends, and population-level research suitable for student projects.

Literature Review

Structured and narrative literature reviews: topic selection, search protocols, evidence synthesis, and producing publishable review manuscripts.

Ancillary Research

Editorial Board

Oversees manuscript quality, formatting, and review process. Provides students editorial training and critical evaluation skills. Ensures smooth functioning of the student research journal.

IRB Facilitation

Guides students in preparing ethical approval applications. Coordinates with institutional boards for review processes. Ensures compliance with ethical standards in research.

Ethics & Review

Promotes integrity and ethical conduct in research activities. Reviews proposals for adherence to ethical norms. Builds awareness of research ethics among members.

Journal

Manages the student research journal’s publication process. Collaborates with editorial and graphics domains. Provides a platform for showcasing student research work.

Organizational Domain

E-Magazine & Publications

Publishes society updates, research highlights, and creative content. Engages members through regular magazine issues. Promotes scientific communication and outreach.

Graphics & Tech

Designs visuals, infographics, and digital media for SRC. Supports research presentations with technical expertise. Ensures attractive and effective communication materials.

Data & Logistics

Manages society records, databases, and data storage. Ensures smooth logistics for events and projects. Provides analytical support to research teams.

Outreach & Collaboration

Builds partnerships with institutions and external societies. Promotes SRC activities through external engagement. Expands networking and collaborative research opportunities.

Human Resource Management (HRM)

Recruits, trains, and manages society members. Ensures active participation and skill development. Maintains harmony and productivity within the society.

Conference & Events

Organizes academic events, workshops, and conferences. Coordinates decor and event execution with volunteers. Showcases SRC’s work on broader academic platforms.

Our Mission & Goals

Fostering a culture of research, innovation, and academic excellence. Equip future healthcare professionals with the skills, ethics, and confidence to lead impactful research in medicine and public health.
