Our Domains

Research Director

This domain will oversee all research related matters of SRC including mentorship of the students in their research projects, IRB facilitation, Editorial board, ethics and review board and a student research journal.

IRB Facilitator

Guides students in preparing ethical approval applications. Coordinates with institutional boards for review processes. Ensures compliance with ethical standards in research.

Editorial Board

Oversees manuscript quality, formatting, and review process. Provides students editorial training and critical evaluation skills. Ensures smooth functioning of the student research journal.

Ethics & Review

Promotes integrity and ethical conduct in research activities. Reviews proposals for adherence to ethical norms. Builds awareness of research ethics among members.

Journal

Manages the student research journal’s publication process. Collaborates with editorial and graphics domains. Provides a platform for showcasing student research work

Frequently Asked Questions

How can I join the Student Research Council? +

Students can apply through our online application portal. We accept applications twice a year and evaluate candidates based on academic performance, research interest, and commitment to our mission.

About SMC SRC

The Student Research Council at Sargodha Medical College promotes research excellence and innovation among medical students through mentorship, resources, and collaborative opportunities.
