Frequently Asked Questions

How do I join a committee?

To join a committee, visit our "Domains" section and click "Apply Now" on the committee you're interested in. Fill out the application form and our team will contact you.

When are SRC elections held?

SRC elections are held annually in the first week of October. All registered students are eligible to vote for their representatives.

How can I report an issue to the SRC?

You can report issues via email at official.srcsmc@gmail.com, through our suggestion box in the cafeteria, or by contacting any SRC representative directly.

Does the SRC organize orientation for new students?

Yes, the SRC organizes a comprehensive orientation program for all new students at the beginning of the academic year to help them adjust to college life.
